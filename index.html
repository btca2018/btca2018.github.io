BTCA 2018

International Workshop on Blockchain Technology for Collaborative Applications

In Conjunction with the 4th IEEE International Conference on Collaboration and Internet Computing (CIC 2018)
Oct 18-20, 2018. Philadelphia, Pennsylvannia, USA

Overview

Blockchain is a constantly evolving state-of-the-art technology that is increasingly finding interest and adoption in a variety of industries including banking, finance, law, insurance, healthcare, government, and supply chain management. While the Internet-based infrastructures, technologies and applications have enabled cross-organizational collaborations and interactions for the past few decades, blockchain has the potential to further revolutionize collaboration in multi-organizational settings. The aim of this workshop is to provide a forum for students, researchers and practitioners from academia and industry to present, share and discuss their research efforts in the use of blockchain technology for collaboration.

We are interested in papers that explore the role, opportunities and challenges of blockchain in collaborative applications and services. Topics of interest include but are not limited to:

  • Security, privacy and trust
  • Smart contract management
  • Collaborative applications and services
  • Blockchain infrastructure management
  • Social computing
  • Legal and policy implications
  • Case studies, proof of concept prototyps

Paper Submission

We invite original research papers that have not been previously published and are not currently under review for publication elsewhere. Submitted papers should be no longer than 8 pages in two-column IEEE proceeding format.
